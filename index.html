Discover the communication between Mars and Earth in different scenarios!
To get started, choose the scenario you want to explore the communication in!

Scenario 1: Conjunction

About every two years, a conjunction, meaning when the sun is between earth and mars for about 2 weeks, is occurring. This makes communication difficult between the two planets. In this scenario the Earth-Mars distance is about 2,5 AU

Scenario 2: Far away

Mars can be as far as 2,5 AU away from earth (during conjunction). 1 AU is approximately equal to 150 million km (Sun-Earth distance). Explore how the communication works when mars isn’t hiding behind the sun but is still far away. In this scenario the distance between the Earth and Mars is about 1,5 AU

Scenario 3: Close to each other

The opposite of scenario 1, Mars is closest to earth every two years. Does this make communication between Earth and Mars easier? In this scenario Earth-Mars distance is about 0,6 AU

Send a message of your choice, and see how the transmission works in space!
